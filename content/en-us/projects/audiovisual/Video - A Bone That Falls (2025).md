# Role

Production/Direction

# Synopsis

A bone inexplicably falls from the sky, and a dog starts devouring it, unaware of where it came from or what it might mean.

# Context

_"A Falling Bone"_ is an experimental video made in a multispecies collaboration with Nemo, a stray dog adopted by my cousins Lívia and Maria Clara.

We produced it at the end of the year using a Canon T5i and the kit lens (18-55mm f/3.5-5.6), and it was a great way to get back into video editing. Now, the video is part of @festivaldominuto – FREE THEME edition (DEC/JAN/FEB 2024-25).
