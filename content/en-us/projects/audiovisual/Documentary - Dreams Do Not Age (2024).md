# Role

Sound Technician

# Context

The film _"Dreams Don't Grow Old"_, directed by Luciana Miguel, was produced during the Basic Cinema training at Fábrica Audiovisual in Santos-SP, taught by Rodrigo Samia, and was made in partnership with Fábrica da Dança and Fábrica da Longevidade, both located in Santos-SP.

# Synopsis

Three elderly women relive their old dream of dancing Classical Ballet through a social project aimed at senior citizens.
