# Role

Director, direct sound, editing, and distribution

# Synopsis

This video is a selection of poems written and recorded by Ademir Demarchi, reflecting on his experience living in the city of Santos. The poems can be found in the book _"Pirão de Sereia"_, published by Realejo Edições, and produced with the Cultural Support Program of the Municipality of Santos in 2010.

# Context

The video was produced as a counterpart to the Aldir Blanc Law award - Alcides Mesquita Prize, granted by the Municipality of Santos and the Federal Government.
